THE GAME STRUCTURE


  • There are 25 players (including you), who live in the same apartment building.
  • Each player gets 10 energy tokens (worth $0.10 each) at the start of each month. Each token represents 1 unit of energy use beyond your basic needs.
  • You will play 6 rounds (for each "month‚Äù of a year). In each month, every player faces the same monthly decision problem: how much energy to keep in your private account versus how much to conserve (by putting it in the group conservation account ).
  • To meet the 60% (=900 tokens) energy reduction goal, each player should donate an average of 6 energy tokens every month to the group conservation account.
