INSTRUCTIONS: GAMEPLAY

  • In each month all players are asked simultaneously: "How much energy do you want to conserve this month?" You may choose to conserve anywhere from 0 to 10 of your monthly energy tokens, keeping the other tokens in your private account.
  • After each month (once all players have made their choices) the month's results will be displayed to all players. You'll get updates on the group conservation account, other player's contributions, and your private account. Players' feedback to the group is anonymous.
  • At the end of the game (after 6 months) each player will be paid based on the rules described on the next page.
