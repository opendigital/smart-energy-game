THE GAME PLAY


  • In each month all players are asked simultaneously: “How much energy do you want to conserve this month?” You may choose to conserve anywhere from 0 to 10 energy tokens each month, keeping the other tokens to your private account.

  • After each month (once all players have made their choices) the month’s results be displayed to all players. You’ll get updates on the group conservation account and your private account. Your personal decision will not be shared with other players.

  • At the end of the game (after 6 months) each player will be paid based on the rules described on the next page.
