INTRODUCTION


Many communities are trying to use less energy. In this experiment, you will play a household energy conservation challenge. It is a real financial investment game where you will make decisions with 24 other people.

As residents of a shared apartment building you are trying to reduce your combined energy usage by 60% (900 tokens). You can earn a smaller or larger financial payment at the end of the game, depending on your and others’ energy choices.

Next, you will get instructions for the game, a trial game and a comprehension quiz (with an opportunity to earn extra income). Then, you’ll proceed to the actual investment game. To make this experiment a success, please read the following rules very attentively.
