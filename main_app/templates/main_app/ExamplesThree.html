EXAMPLE 4: JUST OTHERS CONSERVE​


If you keep ALL of your tokens in your private account, while others contribute ALL of their tokens to group conservation account, you’ll earn more than others:

  • Private account You have 120 tokens left $12.00.
  • Group conservation account The group has 2,520 tokens ($252.00). The group meets the conservation goal, so a bonus is paid by doubling the 2,520 tokens (to 5,040 tokens) and dividing them equally among all players. Every player’s bonus is 229 tokens ($22.90).
  • Your total earnings: $12.00 (private account) + $22.90 (group conservation bonus) = $34.90
  • Pollution reduction : The dollar value of the energy tokens in the conservation account ($252.00) is donated to Carbonfund.org, reducing approximately 55,440 pounds of air pollution

EXAMPLE 5: JUST YOU CONSERVE​​


Or, if you conserve ALL of your tokens, while others keep ALL of theirs in their private accounts, then you’ll earn nothing, while others get: $1.20​

  • Private account You have 0 tokens left $0.00.
  • Group conservation account The group has 120 tokens ($12.00). The group does not meet the conservation goal, so no bonus is paid. Every player’s bonus is 0tokens ($0.00).
  • Your total earnings: $0.00 (private account) + $0.00 (group conservation bonus) = $0.00
  • Pollution reduction : The dollar value of the energy tokens in the conservation account ($12.00) is donated to Carbonfund.org, reducing approximately 2,640 pounds of air pollution
