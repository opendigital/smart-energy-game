EXAMPLE 1: EQUAL 30% CONSERVATION​


If all players conserve 3 energy tokens every month on average, then earnings will follow the image at right:​

  • Private account: Each player has 90 tokens left, earning them $0.90.
  • Group conservation account: has 216 tokens ($2.16). The group meets the conservation goal, so a bonus is paid by doubling the 216 tokens (to 432) and dividing them equally among all players. Every player’s bonus is 72 tokens ($0.72).
  • Your total earnings: $0.90 (private account) + $0.72 (group conservation bonus) = $1.62
  • Pollution reduction: The dollar value of the energy tokens in the conservation account ($2.16) is donated to Carbonfund.org, reducing approximately 475 pounds of air pollution
