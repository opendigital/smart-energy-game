EXAMPLE 1: EQUAL 60% CONSERVATION​


If all players conserve 6 energy tokens every month on average, then the earnings will be as follows

  • Private account : Each player has 48 tokens left, earning them $4.80.
  • Group conservation account : The group has 1,584 tokens ($158.40). The group meets the conservation goal, so a bonus is paid by doubling the 1,584 tokens (to 3,168 tokens) and dividing them equally among all players. Every player’s bonus is 144 tokens ($14.40).
  • Your total earnings: $14.40 (private account) + $14.40 (group conservation bonus) = $19.20
  • Pollution reduction : The dollar value of the energy tokens in the conservation account ($158.40) is donated to Carbonfund.org, reducing approximately 3500 pounds of air pollution
