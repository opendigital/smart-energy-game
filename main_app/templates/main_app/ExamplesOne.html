EXAMPLE 1: EQUAL 60% CONSERVATION​


If all players conserve 6 energy tokens every month on average, then the earnings will be as follows

  • Private account : Each player has 24 tokens left, earning them $2.40.
  • Group conservation account : The group has 900 tokens ($90.00). The group meets the conservation goal, so a bonus is paid by doubling the 900 tokens (to 1,800 tokens) and dividing them equally among all players. Every player’s bonus is 72 tokens ($7.20).
  • Your total earnings: $2.40 (private account) + $7.20 (group conservation bonus) = $9.60.
  • Pollution reduction : The dollar value of the energy tokens in the conservation account ($90.00) is donated to Carbonfund.org, reducing approximately 20,000 pounds of air pollution.
